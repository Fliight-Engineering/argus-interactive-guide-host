### Camera Adjustment

<div class="card-content">

Cameras can be adjusted to face nearly any position using the socket mount. The adjustable pivot allows you to:
- Tilt the camera up or down
- Rotate the camera left or right
- Position for optimal viewing angle

Adjust the camera position before securing the mount to ensure the best view of the required area.
